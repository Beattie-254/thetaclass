Welcome to X-TREME foods

Welcome to X-TREME Foods, your number one source for the best local and international dishes. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of meals, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2016 by Beatrice Adhiambo, X-TREME-foods has come a long way from its beginnings in Mavoko ,Machakos county. When X-TREME foods first started out, her passion for helping other people get food at the comfort of their location drove her to do intense research, quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online online restaurant. We now serve customers all over the country, and are thrilled to be a part of theis time saving industry. We hope you enjoy our foods as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
